Travel & Accomodation

Flying in ✈

Fly into SEA (Seattle-Tacoma International Airport). We recommend staying for the weekend!

Lodging 🏨

HOTEL BLOCK
We have reserved a block at University District Silver Cloud Inn. Call and ask about the family event rate for Jeff and Or's wedding.

AirBnB
AirBnB is cheaper and will probably get you a more fun location. Recommended neighborhoods are Capitol Hill, University District and Belltown
* Group housing is cost-effective and fun! Contact us if you'd like to meet some new wedding guest friends

Getting around 🚗

CAR | 🚗
The simplest option is of course - to rent a car. It will allow you the flexibility to go to the wedding venue, our house, and hikes outside the city. Inside the city, easy and cheap parking may be hard to come by and we would recomend to try to use the public transportation available for your convenience.

PUBLIC TRANSIT | 🚈/🚍
Seattle has an extensive public transportation system. An ORCA card is $5 and you can charge it with money online to use buses and light rail it is available at the airport or online

BIKE | 🚴
Seattle has a bikeshare program called Pronto. Pronto keyfob is $2.50, plus $8/day for unlimited 30 minute rides around downtown and nearby neighborhoods

UBER | 🚕
Uber has a large presence in Seattle and can get you to and from the wedding venue (and anywhere else).Use this link for a free first ride.

WALKING | 🚶
Seattle has plenty of hills if you're looking for a workout. Check out some cool stairway walks in Seattle. Fact: It's only a 5 hour walk to the venue from downtown.

Day Itineraries

Food

  • Coffee

    • El Diablo: Get the Cubano sandwich with a Cortadito drink. Special bonus points for eating where Or proposed to Jeff.

  • Brunch

    • Toulouse Petit: great Cajun food
    • 5 Spot: Fun diner with rotating thematic menues

  • Lunch

    • Enjoy some “beach time” at Bongos (Cuban). Order some Maduras.
    • Aloha Ramen: One of a kind family owned Hawaiian Ramen
    • Thai Tom: very small Thai place makes food right in front of you!

  • Dinner

    • Le pichet: small, romantic, French, Jeff and Or’s first date. Recommended by Alton Brown.
    • Jai Thai: Very good Thai food at the heart of Fremont
    • Mezcalaria Oaxaca: best of Mexico! Try the Mezcal flight.

  • Dessert

    • Molly Moon's ice cream
